Introduction and Problem Identification

Ah, the joys of home improvement. We've all been there, staring at our kitchen and wondering, "What if?". Well, we were given a challenge by Home Depot to reimagine the kitchen remodeling experience. We asked ourselves three questions:
1. How can we ignite customer creativity and bridge the gap between online inspiration and designing with The Home Depot?
2. How can we boost customer confidence and ensure a seamless journey from start to finish, minimizing abandonment along the way?
3. How can we keep Home Depot customers engaged, and what insights can we provide to inform associates about customers and their journey?

User Interview Quotes

"As a homeowner, I find it overwhelming to start a remodeling project. I need a simple, user-friendly solution that can help me visualize the changes."

- Jessica, Home Depot customer

Research and Findings

Our research showed that 44% of people working from home are investing more time in home improvement, and this trend is expected to continue. Additionally, 72% of consumers who have increased their use of omni-channel services expect to maintain this level of utilization (source: Accenture COVID-19 Consumer Research). Simplifying the design process for Home Depot customers builds loyalty, and that loyalty translates to revenue.

In going through the existing customer experience online, we knew we could do better to engage more thoughtfully and strategically with the customers that utilize The Home Depot’s software.

Proposed Solution and Design Features

We wanted to leverage space-intelligent computer vision technology and user assist to accurately measure and replicate a customer's space. This would include placing doors, windows, appliances, and eventually sensing paint colors and room textures to improve data capture and recommendations. We incorporated AR technology to connect customers live with an Interior Designer, allowing them to view and adjust the room design and see new products in their own space.

Prototype Creation

We developed a prototype of the Home Depot Room Scanner, creating an immersive experience that enabled analytics, rich session data, and customer preference data. We combined this design data with a customer data management platform to produce style and design-based remarketing campaigns.

Competitor Analysis

After researching competitors in the home improvement space, we found that our proposed solution offered a more personalized and engaging customer experience. Our Home Depot Room Scanner stood out with its advanced AR technology and seamless integration of the customer's journey.

Feedback and Validation

We received positive feedback from Home Depot associates, mentors, and colleagues on our approach to simplifying the design process. Usability testing demonstrated that the design was user-friendly and effective in addressing the target customers' pain points.

Success Metrics and Monetization Strategies

The results speak for themselves:
‍
‍40% increase in registrations
‍76% increase in project completion rate
‍20% increase in add-to-cart rate.

We were able to demonstrate that our solution not only engaged customers but also drove significant revenue growth for Home Depot.
